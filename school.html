My School

My school name is Mission Girls High School, the oldest traditional educational institution of the century, and is situated in the western part of Rajshahi city, adjacent to the court in the area surrounded by many trees. Environment The Church of Bangladesh is situated on 122 khata or 2.013 acres of land on the mission campus of the Rajshahi Deanery. On the south side of this school, the premises of Shaheed Minar, the office of the Superintendent of Police, the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and the court, are located on the west side. Also, Nawabganj Road passes through the south side of this school.The reputed school was established in 1888 by missionaries of the Presbyterian Church as a primary school to promote female education in the region. In 1967, the school was upgraded to Junior High School. In 1969, it was converted to middle school, and in 1985, it was included in MPO. Since its inception, the school has been spreading the light of education in society. In 1976, a student named Kawsar Jahan from this school participated in the SSC examination in the science department and secured the first position in the Rajshahi Education Board. Moreover, the students who passed from this school are well established in different levels of society, including doctors and engineers.The current president of the managing committee of this century-old holy school run by the Church of Bangladesh, Right Rev. Under the able leadership of Hemen Halder (Bishop, Kushtia Diocese) and the efficient management of Principal Maria Alka Mandal and Assistant Principal Md. Mainul Islam, the activities of the school gained momentum. New Education Curriculum for Postgraduate Excellence Skilled and trained teachers and staff are making relentless efforts in implementation.The school currently has a beautiful library, well-equipped science lab and a highly equipped modern digital lab with 17 laptops, 01 projector, 01 router, 01 scanner, 2 printers and 1 color printer donated by the Ministry of Information and Communication Technology, Government of Bangladesh. The digital lab is named Named after Sheikh Russell, the youngest son of the Father of the Nation, "Sheikh Russell Digital Lab," designated by the Ministry.
